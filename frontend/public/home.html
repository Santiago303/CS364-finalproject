Who We Are

Our Story

Founded in 2010, BookSmart began as a dream of creating a literary sanctuary in the heart of Colorado Springs. What started as a small family-owned bookshop has grown into a beloved community hub for book lovers of all ages.

Our founders, James and Sarah Mitchell, both Colorado natives and lifelong book enthusiasts, wanted to create a space where the joy of reading could be shared and celebrated. Their passion for literature and community building continues to guide everything we do at BookSmart today.

Over the years, we've expanded our original location to include a cozy café, dedicated children's section, and event space for author readings and book clubs, all while maintaining our commitment to personalized service and carefully curated selections.

What We Do

At BookSmart, we're more than just a bookstore. We're a community gathering place, a resource for educational materials, and a champion for literacy in Colorado Springs.

Our store features a thoughtfully selected collection spanning all genres, from bestselling fiction and non-fiction to rare finds and local Colorado authors. We pride ourselves on our knowledgeable staff who provide personalized recommendations based on your interests.

Beyond selling books, we host regular events including author signings, book clubs, children's story times, and writing workshops. Our Mountain Brew Café offers locally-sourced coffee and treats, making BookSmart the perfect place to spend an afternoon with a good book.

Our Values

Literary Excellence

We curate high-quality collections that span diverse genres, authors, and perspectives to enrich our readers' lives.

Community Focus

We create a welcoming space that fosters connection, conversation, and a shared love of reading in Colorado Springs.

Knowledge Sharing

We believe in the power of books to educate, inspire, and transform individuals and communities.

Local Pride

We celebrate Colorado authors, stories, and culture while supporting our local community.
